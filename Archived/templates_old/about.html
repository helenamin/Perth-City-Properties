Aim

The aim of this project is to assist potential buyers and investors to get a better predicted property price.

  • Utilising Machine Learning Model to train existing dataset for better accuracy
  • Creates a visualised story board, enabling potential buyers and investors to explore the properties in the City of Perth

Future Developments

  • To expand the current scope to include more suburbs around WA or even Australia
  • Train Machine Learning model for Apartments and Units. This was not included in the current project as there are many factors involve in determining the price for an Apartment and Unit such as views, building and location
  • Include school catchments area, amenities such as shops, public transports to improve predictions
